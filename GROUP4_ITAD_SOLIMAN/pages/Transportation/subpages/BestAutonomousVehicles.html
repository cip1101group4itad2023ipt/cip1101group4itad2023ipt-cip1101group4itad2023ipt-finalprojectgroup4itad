Best Autonomous Vehicles




2023 Tesla Model S

“The Tesla Model S is one of the groundbreakers in semi-autonomous technology, although the company’s approach has been a bit controversial at times. Like every new Tesla, the Model S comes standard with Tesla’s Autopilot suite of features, which includes lane-departure warning, lane-keep assist and adaptive cruise control.”


"An Enhanced Autopilot system costs $6,000 and adds partially autonomous lane-change assist, partially autonomous highway driving, and park assist. It also includes the Summon feature, which brings the car from its parking spot to you, and Smart Summon, which allows you to remotely move the Model S from its current spot to a location of your choice."

2023 Cadillac Escalade

"With ample horsepower and cutting-edge technology, the Cadillac Escalade is a top choice if you're looking for a large, luxurious SUV. It has appealing infotainment features and spacious accommodations in all three rows. The Escalade is available with GM's Super Cruise system. Super Cruise facilitates hands-free driving on compatible roads."


"The Premium Luxury is the least expensive trim with Super Cruise availability; the Super Cruise package adds $2,500 to this trim's price. Note that the Super Cruise package also requires you to add a $1,500 package that includes three years of OnStar and connected services."

2023 Genesis G90

“The fully redesigned Genesis G90 is a top choice among full-size luxury cars thanks to its value-oriented pricing, gorgeous design, comfortable ride and extensive standard features.”


“Genesis calls its system Highway Driving Assist, and it comes standard on the G90, along with lots of other safety tech. Highway Driving Assist combines adaptive cruise control and lane-centering assist along specific stretches of highway below speeds of 95 mph. This system requires you to keep your hands on the steering wheel for it to operate.”

2023 Ford F-150

"Ford's F-150 is one of the best full-size pickups, offering impressive towing capability and an attractive cabin. The Limited trim is an expensive example of this top-selling truck, but it includes many plush amenities and Ford's BlueCruise semi-autonomous driving technology."


"BlueCruise operates on certain mapped highways by combining adaptive cruise control and lane-centering assist. Under these conditions, the F-150 can stay in its lane and pace a vehicle in front of it with the driver's hands off the wheel."

2023 BMW X5

“The BMW X5 was vital in kicking off the luxury crossover segment. It’s a thriving class, with the X5 being one of the most popular models. This BMW offers comfort and performance, enhanced by many advanced safety features.”


“This SUV is available with the Driving Assistance Professional package ($1,700). This package includes traffic-jam assist and adaptive cruise control; this technology automatically steers the X5 below 40mph in certain highway conditions."

2023 Mercedes-Benz E-Class

"Sophisticated and comfortable, the Mercedes-Benz E-Class has long been an excellent midsize luxury car and a smart buy in the brand’s lineup. It comes standard with features like a 12.3-inch touch screen, remote start and a sunroof."


"While Mercedes supplies several active safety and driver assist features with every E-Class, this car is only capable of semi-autonomous driving if you add some upgrades. The Driver Assistance package ($1,950) provides this capability. It's available on the base E-Class E 350 sedan but also requires the Premium package ($2,400)."

2023 Volvo V90 Cross Country

"The Volvo V90 Cross Country is a luxury wagon that covers many bases. It has a roomy cabin, a poised ride and many standard features."


"Every V90 comes standard with Volvo’s Pilot Assist system among its long list of included safety technology. Pilot Assist provides semi-autonomous driving that includes lane-centering assist and adaptive cruise control. It’s a user-friendly system and is available at speeds up to 80 mph."

2023 Nissan Ariya

“The Ariya joins Nissan's lineup for the 2023 model year. This all-electric crossover has quick acceleration, plenty of upscale technology and an affordable price.”


“Nissan's EV is available with ProPilot Assist 2.0, a feature that facilitates hands-free highway driving. It's standard on the Ariya's Empower+ and higher trims.”

2023 Infiniti QX50

"The Infiniti QX50 is held back in our rankings by its unrefined transmission. However, it has appealing strengths, including affordable pricing and roomy cargo capacity."


"The QX50 comes standard with ProPilot Assist. This system uses a combination of lane-keep assist and adaptive cruise control to keep the vehicle traveling at a steady pace on the highway."

2023 Volkswagen ID.4

"The Volkswagen ID.4 is an affordable all-electric crossover that comes standard with a comprehensive suite of advanced safety features, which Volkswagen calls IQ.Drive. This peppy crossover is one of the best buys in its segment."


"IQ.Drive includes Travel Assist, Volkswagen's semi-autonomous driving system. Travel Assist keeps the ID.4 centered in its lane and maintains a fixed distance from the vehicle ahead. Note that this system only works if both hands are on the wheel."
