Smart Devices: Kitchen Edition



KITCHEN: MUST-HAVE CONNECTED COOKING AND CLEANING TOOLS


Whether you're a master chef or an Uber Eats power user, the best smart kitchen gadgets can bring serious convenience to the heart of your home.

Anova Precision Oven

by Anova

The Anova Precision Oven lets you bake, roast, slow cook, sous vide, and steam food to perfection, making it one the smartest countertop ovens you can buy.

Quart Air Fryer

by cosori

The Cosori Smart 5.8-Quart Air Fryer crisps up fresh and frozen foods in a flash, with little to no added oil, and works with an app and voice commands.

Pot Pro Plus

by instant home

The Instant Pot Pro Plus offers eight different cooking methods, from the brand's classic pressure cooking to sous vide, but its mobile app integration doesn't add much to the overall experience.

IQ Robot Vacuum

by sharkclean

The Shark IQ Robot Self-Empty XL RV1001AE vacuum holds up to a months' worth of dust and debris in its base station, and features a self-cleaning brushroll and lots of smart features for a truly low-maintenance experience.
