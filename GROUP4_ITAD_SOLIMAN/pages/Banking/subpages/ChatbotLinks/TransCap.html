Transactional Capabilities



Account Management


Banking offers a wide range of transactional capabilities for account management. Customers can perform various transactions such as checking account balances, viewing transaction history, transferring funds between accounts, paying bills, and setting up automatic payments. These capabilities provide customers with convenient control over their financial activities and allow them to manage their accounts effectively.


Payments and Transfers


Banking provides secure and efficient payment and transfer capabilities. Customers can initiate electronic funds transfers, make person-to-person payments, set up recurring payments, and conduct international wire transfers. These capabilities enable seamless and timely transactions, allowing customers to send and receive money quickly and conveniently.


Deposit and Withdrawal


Banking offers deposit and withdrawal capabilities to facilitate the movement of funds. Customers can deposit money into their accounts through various channels, such as bank branches, ATMs, or mobile banking applications. Similarly, they can withdraw cash from ATMs or make cash withdrawals at bank branches. These capabilities provide customers with flexibility in managing their funds.


Card Services


Banking provides a range of transactional capabilities related to card services. Customers can activate and deactivate their debit or credit cards, report lost or stolen cards, request card replacements, and update card information. They can also set spending limits, monitor transactions, and receive real-time alerts for card activities. These capabilities ensure the security and convenience of card-based transactions.


Loan and Mortgage Management


Banking enables customers to manage their loans and mortgages conveniently. Customers can view loan or mortgage details, make loan payments, set up automatic loan repayments, and access loan statements. They can also apply for new loans or mortgages and track the status of their applications. These capabilities streamline the loan and mortgage management process, empowering customers to stay on top of their financial commitments.
