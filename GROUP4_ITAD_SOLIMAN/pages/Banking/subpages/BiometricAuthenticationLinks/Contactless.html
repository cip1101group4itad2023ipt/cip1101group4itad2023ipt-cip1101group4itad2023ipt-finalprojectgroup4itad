Contactless Biometrics



Hygienic and Safe Authentication

Contactless biometrics offer a hygienic and safe authentication method in banking. Users can authenticate themselves without physical contact with devices or surfaces, reducing the risk of spreading germs or viruses. This is especially relevant in situations where public health and hygiene are a concern, ensuring the well-being of both customers and banking personnel.

Convenient and User-Friendly Experience

Contactless biometrics provide a convenient and user-friendly experience in banking. Users can authenticate themselves by simply presenting their biometric features, such as fingerprints or palm prints, without the need for physical tokens or manual entry. This streamlined authentication process saves time, enhances user experience, and reduces friction in banking transactions.

Enhanced Security

Contactless biometrics offer enhanced security in banking. By relying on unique biometric features, such as facial recognition or iris scans, it becomes significantly more challenging for fraudsters to replicate or impersonate the user. This helps prevent unauthorized access, identity theft, and fraudulent transactions, ensuring the security of customer accounts and financial data.

Fast and Efficient Transactions

Contactless biometrics enable fast and efficient banking transactions. Users can complete authentication and verification processes within seconds, minimizing the time required for manual entry or physical token-based authentication. This improves the overall speed and efficiency of transactions, benefiting both customers and financial institutions.

Integration with Mobile and Self-Service Channels

Contactless biometrics can seamlessly integrate with mobile banking apps and self-service channels. Users can authenticate themselves using their biometric features on their mobile devices or at self-service kiosks, enabling a secure and convenient banking experience. This integration enhances accessibility and offers flexible banking options to customers.
